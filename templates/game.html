We know your energy can be drained from working so diligently, so why not play a little mini game as you take a break :)

Bad Bunny is a minigame, the object is to click the bunnies away from the carrot in the middle of the screen for as long as you can

Every 10 seconds of survival, the level goes up by one

Every time the level goes up, the speed of the bunnies and the number of bunnies that come after the carrot increases

Your score is the number of seconds you manage to survive
